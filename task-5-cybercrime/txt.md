**phising attack **

A classic and highly relevant case study for students is the 2016 University of Virginia (UVA) Phishing Attack, which perfectly illustrates how a single digital mistake can compromise an entire institution.

The Scenario
In this attack, several employees received a sophisticated "spear-phishing" email that appeared to be from the university’s Human Resources department. The email claimed there was an urgent issue with their payroll or benefits and directed them to a spoofed login page that looked identical to the official UVA portal.

The Impact
Believing the site was legitimate, a small number of employees entered their university credentials. The attackers used these stolen passwords to gain access to the university’s internal systems. They successfully redirected the direct-deposit paychecks of those employees to their own bank accounts. Beyond the financial loss, the breach forced the university to reset thousands of passwords and undergo a massive security audit, causing significant administrative disruption.
